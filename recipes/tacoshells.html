Taco Shells

Description

Recipe found on YouTube. (Simply Mama Cooks)

Makes 14 tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 Cups - All Purpose Floud
  • 2 TSP - Baking Powder
  • 1 1/4 Cup - Hot Water
  • 1/4 Cup - Oil
  • 1 TSP - Salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in mixing bowl.
  2. Add Oil and hot water to mixing bowl.
  3. Mix util moist and slightly tacky
  4. Knead 10-15 minutes, the dough should be smooth and soft.
  5. Rest 10-15 minutes
  6. Roll out tortilla
